Here's a time line of Elon Musk's Life:

  • 1971

    Born in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa.

  • 1983

    At the age of 12, teaches himself computer programming and sold his BASIC-based video game, Biastar, to a magzine called PC and Office Technology for approximate $500 USD.

  • 1995

    Moves to California to begin a PhD in applied physics and materials science at Stanford University, but left to program to pursue entrepreneurial aspirations in areas of internet, renewable energy and outer space.

  • 2002

    Im October 2002, Sold PayPal to eBay for $1.5 billion in stock.

    In June 2002, becomes chief executive officer and chief technology officer of SpaceX, a company that develops and manufactures space launch vehicles with a focus on advancing state of rocket technology.

  • 2003

    Establishes Tesla Motors, an automative and energy storage company that designs, manufactures, and sells electric cars, electric vheicle powertrain components and battery products.

  • 2006

    Helps cousins, Peter and Lyndon Rive start SolarCity, a company that designs, finances and installs solar power systems.

  • 2013

    Unveils Hyperloop, a concept of high-speed transportation system incorportating reduced-pressure tubes in which pressurized capsules ride on an air cushion driven by linear induction motors and air compressors.
